About Us

We are a volunteer fire brigade from Poland. We operate on a voluntary basis.
We provide assistance during: road accidents, fires and natural disasters (fires, storms/windstorms, floods, etc.) We are an association with nearly 100 years of tradition. We have specialized equipment that we use during events. We work together with: the state fire brigade, the police, the ambulance service, and the air ambulance service. We are able to provide assistance 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. We operate in the area of the village, commune, poviat and if necessary, we will even go beyond our area. In our free time, we conduct first aid training for children, teenagers and adults. We take part in festivals and events organized by the local government, where we conduct "chats" on safety and first aid.We are a volunteer organization - firefighters do not receive any remuneration. We are financed by: external companies, grands, funds of the Ministry of Interior and Administration, European programs nad by our commynity .

How You can support us ?

There is a few way to suppor us :

    • 1% of income tax - during the tax settlement period, you can transfer 1% to us. The collected money will be spent on the purchase of equipment for firefighters, which will be used during rescue operations.

      Donation - You can make a donation to our bank account at any time. A good opportunity to provide financial support is also the annual action of distributing firefighting calendars, which takes place in the last two months of the year. We visit the residents of our town, who can give any support to our unit.

      Material support/other types of support - If you have any other idea how you could support us - please contact us. You can also provide us with equipment such as: flashlights, radios, thermal imaging cameras, firefighting shoes, balaclavas, gloves that firefighters will use during their service. Other examples of support can be e.g. car wrecks that will be used for training in technical rescue, or vouchers for such training conducted by external companies.
